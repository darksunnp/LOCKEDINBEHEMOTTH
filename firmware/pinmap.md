# Pin Map

Brief intro paragraph about the board and how power is organized.

## Signal pins
| Function | ESP32-S3 GPIO | Notes |
[TFT MOSI, SCK, CS, D/C, RESET, LED — GPIO11, 12, 10, 8, 9, 46
Encoder A, B, SW — GPIO4, 5, 6
Servo PWM — GPIO14
Buzzer — GPIO17
Power: TFT VCC on +3V3, servo V+ on +5V, all grounds common]

## Power
5V rail

## Unconnected
[SD pins, MISO]
